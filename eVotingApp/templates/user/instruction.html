Federal Election Instruction

  • Please select whether you want elect by senate or individual.
  • Select the election type by selecting the radio button.
  • Accordingly follow the election rules.

Election by Senate rules:

  • Choose the preference you want to vote(max:6 preferences).
  • Type the preference number in the box near the senate name.
  • Start numbering from 1 and end at 6.
  • Always the number 1 is the highest and the preference goes down.

Election by Individual rules:

  • Choose the preference you want to vote(max:12 preferences).
  • Type the preference number in the box near the Individuals name.
  • Start numbering from 1 and end at 12.
  • Always the number 1 is the highest and the preference goes down.
